Halo-Halo

Description


 Halo-Halo is the ultimate pinoy summer treat. It is a layered dessert consisting of sweetened beans, fruits, shaved ice drizzled with evaporated milk, and ice cream

Ingredients


  • 2 tbsp kaong (sugar palm seed)
  • 2 tbsp sweetened langka (jackfruit strips)
  • 2 tbsp sweetened white beans
  • 2 tbsp coconut sport strips (macapuno)
  • 2 tbsp red monggo beans
  • 2 tbsp sweetened plantains (saba)
  • 2 tbsp purple yam jam (ube)
  • 2 tbsp creme caramel custard (leche flan)
  • 2 scoops of ice cream
  • 1 cup shaved ice
  • 1/4 cup evaporated milk
  • 1 tsp white sugar optional

Directions


  1. In a clear glass bowl, place a cup of shaved or crushed ice
  2. Then pour 1/4 cup of evaporated milk
  3. Then add the first nine ingredients respectively
  4. Top two scoops of ice cream at the center
  5. Serve with a long spoon
